## Welcome

Welcome to the online home of the dash.js project. 

dash.js is an open-source, production-quality Javascript framework for building video and audio players that play back [MPEG-DASH](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dynamic_Adaptive_Streaming_over_HTTP) content. 

For more info, see the “What” and “Why” on our [About](about) page.

## Where do I start?

- Web developers. If you want to use dash.js to play MPEG-DASH media streams in your web app, read the [Getting Started for Web Developers](gsfwd) page.
- Contributing. To contribute to the project (code, testing, documentation, anything!), visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Dash-Industry-Forum/dash.js).
- Demo. To see MPEG-DASH streaming in action, check out our demo at [http://dashif.org/reference/players/javascript/index.html].
- Keep in touch. To stay informed about dash.js goings-on, [join our email list](https://groups.google.com/d/forum/dashjs).
